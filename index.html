A Brief Overview of Youtube in 2023

YouTube is one of the world's largest online video-sharing platform, where billions of people create, watch and share videos everyday. It is also one of the most popular social media platforms, with over 2 billion monthly active users. In this visualisation, we will be exploring the data of YouTube videos in 2023. We will be looking at the number of videos uploaded, the number of views, the number of likes and dislikes, and the number of comments. We will also be looking at the most popular categories and the most popular channels.

YouTubers Across the Globe

United States (USA), has one of the most YouTubers across the world, at 295 content creators. Whereas, India has the second most YouTubers, at 270 content creators. With this, we can see that the content creators are spreading across the world, and not only in the Western countries.
